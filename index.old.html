Cardboard is a Fabric mod that adds support for the popular Bukkit/Spigot/Paper modding API.  This mod lets you use plugins that are made for Bukkit and it's derivatives (Spigot & Paper) with Fabric.

Cardboard is still in development and may not support every plugin.
